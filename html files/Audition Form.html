Welcome to the auditions of Entrepreneurship Development Cell, NIT Durgapur!


The form consists of 3 sections:

1. Student Details
2. Core Team
3. Technical Team

It is mandatory for everyone to fill the first and second sections (i.e., Student details and Core part).
If you are auditioning for Technical Team, you need to fill up the core sections as well along with the technical part.

Description of Roles-

Core Team:

i) Designing and conducting events for the promotion of entrepreneurship among the youth. Successfully handling the execution of such events with the help of club mates.
ii) Participating in entrepreneurship related events of other colleges for self development.

Technical Team:

i) To make posters and graphic designs for the club.
ii) To make promotional videos, trailers for different events which is organized by EDC.
iii) Web Development team helps to maintain and/or enhance the current website and contribute to/co-create other initiatives by EDC in the same domain.
iv) To assist the Core team in management as and when required.
